Welcome to Debonair

Quality refrigeration equipment New Zealand wide
Our Products
Debonair refrigerated cabinets have been serving the hospitality industry for more than forty years. During that time, we have seen the development from custom building bottle coolers and keg coolers to order as required, to the creation and stocking a full range of standard sized chillers to meet the needs of all sectors of the hospitality industry. Over the years we have supplied to airlines, tourist shipping, passenger rail and casinos, as well as the more obvious markets in the catering and hospitality industries. All our products use both CFC free refrigerants and insulation for maximum thermal efficiency and environmental protection. Debonair refrigerated cabinets all come with a full two year warranty.
Company History
Debonair Industries was established in 1970 with the start of the Debonair brand name, designs and manufacturing plant. Since then the range of standard chillers manufactured has widened to meet a greater variety of market requirements - we now manufacture more than 200 different models with a full range of options.

The dedicated team at Debonair are committed to manufacturing high quality refrigerated cabinets to meet the demands of the hospitality industry and deliver on time - all the time.
